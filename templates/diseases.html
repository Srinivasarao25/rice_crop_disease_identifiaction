Rice Leaf Diseases

Bacterial Leaf Blast

Remedy: Use resistant varieties and apply copper-based fungicides.

Brown Spot

Remedy: Use fungicides like tricyclazole, and remove infected leaves.

Leaf Blast

Remedy: Use resistant varieties and apply appropriate fungicides.

Leaf Scald

Remedy: Apply fungicides such as azoxystrobin and improve irrigation management.

Narrow Brown Spot

Remedy: Use resistant varieties and proper nitrogen management.
